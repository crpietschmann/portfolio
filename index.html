Bio

Chris Pietschmann is a long-time Microsoft MVP with Azure technologies, Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT), HashiCorp Ambassador (2021), Author, Developer, DevOps / Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) with over 20 years of experience designing and developing enterprise solutions. He has been focusing heavily on Microsoft Azure and the Cloud since early 2010, and specializes in DevOps / SRE, IoT, Development and Solution Architecture. He runs the #1 Microsoft Azure blog at Build5Nines.com, and works at Solliance as a Principal Cloud & DevOps Solution Architect.
